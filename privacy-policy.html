Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

Introduction

At Lowcountry Resale Solutions (“we,” “our,” or “us”), we fully respect and protect the privacy of every customer. Your personal and financial information is never shared, never sold, and never disclosed to any third party under any circumstance, regardless of requests, legal threats, or even claims made by any sort of entity. This is a non-negotiable guarantee that your personal & financial data is protected at all costs..

Information We Collect

We collect only the information absolutly required to securely process your transactions and provide our services:

  • Personal details: Name, email, billing address, phone number.
  • Bank/payment information: Account numbers, routing numbers, or other basic payment credentials.
  • Transaction data: Purchase history and payment confirmations/declines.

How We Use Your Information

  • Processing payments ,completing transactions, managing fraud risk.
  • Sending payment confirmations and receipts.
  • Managing order fulfillment and customer support.
  • Internal reporting and account reconciliation to ensure accurate service.

Absolute Non-Disclosure Policy

We guarantee that your data will never be shared, sold, or exposed to any individual, company, government agency, or third party—no exceptions. Any access to your data is strictly limited to authorized personnel within our company solely for transaction processing and customer support.

Data Security Measures

  • Bank-level encryption for all payment data.
  • Secure servers with restricted access.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring.

While no digital system on earth is 100% immune to cyber threats, we do take every single possible precaution to ensure your data remains private.

Your Rights

You retain full control over your personal and financial data. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Your personal privacy choices are fully respected and enforced without any exceptions.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 15 years of age. We do not knowingly or intentionally collect personal data from children under 17 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new regulations or improvements in data security. Any updates will be posted publicly on our website with the new effective date.
